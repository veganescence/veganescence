About us

A member of The Vegan Society, we are a company that believes that a vegan life can be fragrant and stylish as well as cruelty-free.

Our candles offer a clean, pure burn for a healthier environment, and were developed using sustainably sourced soy wax, and vegan-friendly fragrances.

We use a combination of safe, synthetic ingredients for a stronger scent, essential oils to calm, relax and energise, and wild plants.

We use traditional cotton wicks in our smaller candles and wooden wicks in larger candles that give a pleasant 'crackle' sound when lit.
